About Tim (Jui-Ting) Hsu

Tim Hsu is currently a masters student studying Data Science at NYU. Prior to joining NYU, he received his bachelors degree in both Computer Engineering and Statistics with a minor in Mathematics from University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. He is interested in NLP, machine learning, data visualizations, and other various applications of data science.
